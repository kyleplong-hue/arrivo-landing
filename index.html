Drag-and-Drop Itineraries

Day-by-day plans guests can rearrange. Drag entire days to reorder, or move individual blocks within a day. Color-coded tags — food, wine, activity, relaxation — so they see the shape of their trip at a glance.

↻

Smart "New Idea" Swaps

Don't like a dinner pick? Hit swap. Arrivo suggests a category-matched alternative from your curated recommendations bank. Dinner swaps for dinner, winery for winery — never a random suggestion.

🗺️

Themed Day Trip Routes

Pre-built themed itineraries — Wine & Vineyards, Farm & Food, City Explorer. Each with stop-by-stop timing, drive times, and Google Maps links. Guests pick a theme and go.

📍

One-Tap Maps & Route Planning

Every venue links to Google Maps. A "Route" button on each day opens turn-by-turn directions through all that day's stops. Bilingual addresses for taxi drivers included.

📋

Booking Requests

Guests request restaurant reservations and experience bookings through the app. You confirm or decline from your dashboard — all tracked in one place with status updates.

AI Concierge Chat

An AI concierge trained on your recommendations answers guest questions 24/7. Restaurants, directions, local tips — your expertise, always available. With quick-tap suggestions for common questions.

🎫

Booked Tours in the Itinerary

Guests' booked tours appear right in their day-by-day plan — meeting point, time, confirmation number, guide details. No digging through email. Everything they need for the day, in one place.

Guest Manager

Create personalized guides per guest with their name, dates, and notes. Generate a shareable link in one click. Track all active guests, their itinerary, and booking requests from a single view. Click any guest card to edit details inline.

iCal Reservation Import

Paste your iCal URL from Airbnb, Vrbo, Rezdy, or Booking.com. Arrivo pulls guest names, emails, phone numbers, party size, and reservation codes. Import individually or bulk-import all upcoming bookings. Auto-creates guest entries.

📑

Template System

Build itinerary templates for different trip types — weekend getaway, full week, wine-focused, family-friendly. Set a default template. New guest guides clone from your chosen template in seconds.

📋

Recommendations Bank

Your full library of curated restaurants, wineries, experiences, and local tips — tagged, categorized, and searchable. Filter by dinner, wine, nature, shopping. Add new spots anytime. The engine behind smart swaps.

Inline Editing

Click any recommendation card to edit the name, description, category, maps link, and booking status directly. Edit itinerary templates in the same drag-and-drop Week View your guests use. No separate admin panel needed.

Global Find & Replace

Changed the name of something? Rename across every itinerary and template with global term replacements. One change propagates everywhere — descriptions, day trips, and concierge responses.

For Tour Companies
Plug into your existing booking system.

Connect your Rezdy, FareHarbor, or Peek Pro account. When a guest books a tour, Arrivo automatically generates their personalized pre-trip guide and sends it via email or WhatsApp.

📥 Auto-Import Bookings

iCal sync pulls new reservations every hour. Guest name, dates, party size, contact info — parsed and ready. Booked tours slot directly into the guest's day-by-day itinerary with meeting point, time, and confirmation details.

📧 Auto-Send Guides

When a guest imports, their guide link is ready to send. One-click email with a pre-filled body: "Here's your personalized trip planner." Or automate it via Zapier webhook.

📊 Track Engagement

See which guests opened their guide, what they swapped, what bookings they requested. Know who's engaged before they arrive — and who might need a nudge.
