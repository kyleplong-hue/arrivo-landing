Arrivable — Positioning & Rollout Plan
March 2026 · Kyle Long

Core Positioning

Master headline: "Arrivable delivers branded, personalized itineraries for every guest—turning empty travel time into seamless, high-value experiences."

Segment Positioning

1. Cruise Ship Companies
Guests constantly ask "what should I do here?" across multiple ports → fragmented, repetitive burden on staff.
Long

Arrivable gives every cruise guest a personalized, branded itinerary for every port—before they board, while they're docked, and after they disembark—so you reduce onboard questions and increase excursion and partner revenue.

Short

We turn every port into a curated, personalized experience for your guests—without adding work for your crew.

2. Hotels & Resorts
Dead time (pre-check-in, gaps in schedule), underutilized amenities, repetitive concierge load.
Long

Arrivable gives your guests a personalized daily itinerary—from the moment they land to the moment they leave—so they spend more, do more, and enjoy their stay more.

Short

We turn time before check-in into a fully planned stay—without adding pressure to your concierge team.

3. Luxury Rentals
Guests want guidance, hosts don't scale, experience quality impacts reviews.
Long

Arrivable gives every guest a curated, luxury itinerary for their stay—so your luxury rental feels more like a five-star hotel concierge experience and earns better reviews.

Short

No more "what should we do?". Arrivable creates personalized itineraries for every guest automatically.

4. Executive Travel / Luxury Tour Operators
Gaps before/after tours, brand experience breaks, manual planning overhead.
Long

Arrivable extends your brand beyond the tour—giving clients curated, on-brand itineraries before and after their trip, from the moment they arrive.

Short

Your clients never feel "on their own"—Arrivable fills every gap in their journey with curated, personalized experiences.

Personalization hook (one per email):

  • New room / wing / program launch

Cadence: 3 emails, 4–5 days apart

Email Templates — Hotels & Luxury Lodges

Email 2 (Day 4) — Subject: what the numbers look like
Quick follow-up on Arrivable. For a 20-suite property running 2–4 night stays, the pre-arrival window is the highest-leverage moment to increase revenue per guest — but most lodges are handling it over email, which means the best add-ons never get seen. Arrivable surfaces them in a clean, visual format that's on-brand and easy for guests to act on. The average lift in ancillary bookings (spa, dining, experiences) is significant — and staff email volume drops at the same time. Happy to show you what it would look like for [Property] specifically. 20 minutes?
Email 3 (Day 10) — Subject: closing the loop
