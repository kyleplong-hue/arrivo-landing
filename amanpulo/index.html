The problem

$1,500 a night. A PDF brochure and a generic email.

Ultra-luxury hotels charge extraordinary rates — and then send guests a wall of text, a link to a PDF, and hope they figure out what to book. The experiences team is left fielding WhatsApp messages at midnight. Most guests don't pre-book anything. Revenue is left on the table.

The first impression should match the product. It doesn't.

Current pre-arrival email — Amanpulo
Dear Mr. Long,
Mabuhay and greetings from Amanpulo! On behalf of Amanpulo's experiences team, we wish you well and look forward to creating memorable experiences for your upcoming stay...

We invite you to discover the many activities that await in the Guest Service Directory, please click here. We encourage pre-booking their activities, so their time on the island can be dedicated to simply savouring every moment...
↑ A link to a 68-page PDF. Zero personalisation. No package recognition. No way to book directly.
The solution

A digital concierge that knows who your guest is before they arrive.

01
Package-aware itinerary builder
Guests see their included package experiences pre-loaded and marked. Add-ons are displayed with real-time pricing and availability. A live folio updates with their estimated charges at check-out.
02
One-click staff confirmation
The experiences team sees all incoming requests in a single dashboard — guest name, package, requested experiences, notes, and a confirm button that notifies the guest instantly.
03
White-labelled, instantly beautiful
Delivered as a personalised link in the pre-arrival email. Branded to your property. Every guest gets a version that reflects their stay — accommodation, package, special occasions, dietary notes.
